Stocky Rice

Quick and easy stocky rice!

This is my favorite recipe on a lazy night! A real comfort food.

This soup-stock rice is a quick set-and-forget recipe that will always have you coming back for more!

Ingredients

  • Your favorite rice. Mine is basmati.
  • Soup stock of your choice. Mine is chicken.
  • Fresh onions
  • Rice cooker

Steps

  1. Wash your rice. Seriously, wash your rice. 3 times at least, or until the water runs CLEAR.
  2. Measure your water. Remember how much rice you washed, your rice cooker should have lines to show you how much water to add. Add that much HOT water.
  3. Dissolve stock. Stir your stock around in the hot water until dissolved.
  4. Add rice. Add your WASHED rice to the soup stock water.
  5. Wait. Turn on your rice cooker and wait! It does the cooking for you!
  6. Enjoy! After 20 minutes or so, your rice will be done! Be careful though, it's hot, so let it sit for a few minutes before you eat.
